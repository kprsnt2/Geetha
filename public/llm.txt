# Geetha — Bhagavad Gita Daily Shloka Platform
# URL: https://geetha.kprsnt.in
# Contact: https://github.com/kprsnt2/Geetha

## About
Geetha is a free, bilingual (English & Telugu) platform to read the Bhagavad Gita one verse per day.
All 700 shlokas are available in Sanskrit with English and Telugu translations.
Each verse comes with transliteration, commentary, and AI-generated spiritual insights connecting ancient wisdom to modern life.

## Content
- 700 Bhagavad Gita verses in Sanskrit, English, and Telugu
- Daily verse rotation with progress tracking
- AI-powered spiritual blog posts with life lessons
- Transliteration for each verse
- 18 chapters covering dharma, karma, bhakti, jnana, and moksha
